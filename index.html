About us

At B&E Carpentry, we take great pride in crafting exceptional woodworking solutions that bring your vision to life.
With over 20 years of experience in the carpentry industry, we thrive in delivering customized, top-notch craftsmanship to our clients.
Founded by Bernardo Melendez, a passionate and dedicated carpenter, our business began as a one-person venture with a simple goal - to provide unparalleled carpentry services that exceed expectations.
Over the years, we have built a reputation for excellence in Houston and surrounding areas by working hard and staying committed to quality.
